Gommage Extraordinaire is a sugar ester emulsion that progressively changes into an oil during the massage releasing its natural exfoliation and a slight nourishing heated sensation to reveal smoother, nourished skin. Enriched with Australian fruits complex like tropical AHAs and macadamia extract, Gommage Extraordinaire can be applied as a mask for regenerated and radiant skin. Its intoxicating bespoke natural fragrance calms the senses for a relaxing self-care ritual.

Macadamia seeds powder, combined with natural sand and organic sugar gently exfoliates, eliminating impurities and irregularities caused by dead skin cells to reveal soft, supple, smooth skin. It helps to moisturize and accelerates skin desquamation to fade away uneven skin pigmentation.

Enriched with mineral-rich seawater and Palmaria Palmata seaweed extract, it gently cleanses and stimulates cell renewal. It acts as a tightener and purifier to gently capture and eliminate all impurities including polluting particles and sebum with a formula that combines the benefits of vegetable charcoal with natural exfoliants. The expert blend contains tropical fruit extracts grown in Australia including mango, pineapple and papaya. Its complex is rich in proteolytic enzymes and works as an antioxidant to fight free radicals and protect your skin from aging. Use before Eclat Extraordinaire in your skincare routine to buff away dead skin cells for fresh, revitalized skin that is ready for hydration from Eclat Extraordinaire.

Apply a thin layer to clean, dry face and neck, avoiding the eye area. Leave for at least 10 minutes. Massage in circular motions with lightly moistened hands until the emulsion turns into delicately scented milky bath. Rinse with water. Use 1 or 2 times a week to buff away dead skin cells gently. This ritual may be followed with a thin layer of the Eclat Extraordinaire for a healthy and hydrated glow.

Australian Macadamia extract, Vegetable charcoal (from silica rich Bamboo), Natural exfoliants (French quartz sand powder, olive stones from Provence), enriched with pure seawater and French Red Algae (Palmaria seaweed) extract, it provides a wealth of magnesium, potassium, calcium, trace elements with balancing, purifying and remineralizing virtues.

Gommage Extraordinaire is 100% natural, clinically proven, COSMOS certified and made in France as part of La Fervance’s Fusionistique Skincare.
